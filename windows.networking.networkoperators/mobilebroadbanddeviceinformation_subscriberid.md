For devices that support the CDMA cellular class, the [SubscriberId](mobilebroadbanddeviceinformation_subscriberid.md) value represents the Mobile Identification Number (MIN) string, or the International Roaming MIN (IRM), which is 10 digits long.## -examples## -see-also
