# Windows.Networking.NetworkOperators.ESim.Policy

## -description
Gets the policy for the eSIM, regarding whether the Local User Interface (LUI) for eSIM management should be enabled or disabled.

## -property-value
The policy for the eSIM, regarding whether the LUI should be enabled or disabled.

## -remarks
LUI is defined in [SGP.22](https://www.gsma.com/newsroom/all-documents/sgp-22-v2-2-technical-specification) (the published eSIM standard).
